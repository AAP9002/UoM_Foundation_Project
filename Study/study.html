Introduction

Transitioning from A levels to university can be intimidating, especially with all the pressure put on your studies. You may feel unsure or confused as to what you’ll have to do in your next year of education, or perhaps you’d like to just get a head start on the game. Whatever your reasons, this guide will have you covered with a comprehensive overview of what to expect on the Foundation Year Course.
