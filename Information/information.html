Introduction

At the University of Manchester, the foundation course provides you with the necessary skills and knowledge to be able to do your desired course in the first year. This is done within a foundation year which is essentially your first accademitc . The foundation course provides a plethora of progression courses with them being:

  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Bioscience
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Chemistry
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer science
  • Computer System Engineering
  • Earth and Planetary Sciences
  • Electrical, Electronic and Mechatronic Engineering
  • Environmental Science
  • Materials Scicenc
  • Mathematics
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Medicine
  • Pharmacy
  • Physics

Accepting to complete a foundation year will mean you will be in University for 4 years (and 6 years if you opted for medicine)

Computer Science (Overview)

Within the foundation year, you can choose The course is taught at The University, by academics from The University. Provided you achieve the specific progression criteria for your chosen degree, completion of the Integrated Foundation Year guarantees you a place on first year of your chosen degree. In the first semester, you will have a choice between doing 2 physics subjects or 1 physics and 1 chemistry along with maths being compolsary to provide you the student with enough information to help you excell in your first year.
